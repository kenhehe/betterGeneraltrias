# Join Disaster Drills and Evacuation Planning

The City Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office (CDRRMO) of General Trias City conducts regular disaster drills and community evacuation planning activities to prepare residents for emergencies, particularly given the city's proximity to Taal Volcano.

## Types of Drills Conducted

- **Earthquake drill** — "Duck, Cover, Hold" and evacuation procedures
- **Volcanic eruption drill** — evacuation routes, ash fall response
- **Fire drill** — evacuation and fire suppression basics
- **Flood/lahar drill** — applicable to low-lying barangays near Taal Lake

## How to Participate

1. Watch for announcements from your barangay hall or CDRRMO
2. Schools, offices, and community groups may request a drill by contacting CDRRMO
3. Attend the scheduled drill at your workplace, school, or community

## Evacuation Planning

Your barangay has a designated **Evacuation Center**. Know yours before a disaster strikes. Evacuation maps are posted at barangay halls.

**Key Evacuation Centers in General Trias:**

- Tolentino Sports and Activity Center (TSAC)
- Designated school grounds and covered courts per barangay

## Tips for Residents

- Know your evacuation route and meeting point
- Prepare a "Go Bag" with essentials (water, food, documents, medicines)
- Sign up for the city's early warning SMS alerts at your barangay hall
