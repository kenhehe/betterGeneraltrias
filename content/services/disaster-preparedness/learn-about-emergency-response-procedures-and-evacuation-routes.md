# Learn About Emergency Response Procedures and Evacuation Routes

General Trias City has established emergency response procedures and evacuation routes due to its location near Taal Volcano. Knowing these procedures can save lives during disasters.

## Evacuation Routes

Main evacuation routes from General Trias City lead to:

- **Silang, Cavite** — via Emilio Aguinaldo Highway going north
- **Alfonso, Cavite** — via the Alfonso-General Trias Road
- **Amadeo** — via the interior roads

**Avoid routes going toward Taal Lake** during volcanic alerts.

## Alert Levels (Taal Volcano — PHIVOLCS)

| Level         | Meaning                        | Action                        |
| ------------- | ------------------------------ | ----------------------------- |
| Alert Level 1 | Low-level unrest               | Monitor; no evacuation        |
| Alert Level 2 | Increasing unrest              | Prepare; avoid danger zone    |
| Alert Level 3 | Relatively high unrest         | Evacuate 14-km radius         |
| Alert Level 4 | Hazardous eruption imminent    | Evacuate extended danger zone |
| Alert Level 5 | Hazardous eruption in progress | Full evacuation               |

## What to Do During an Eruption

1. Follow PHIVOLCS and CDRRMO advisories
2. Evacuate immediately when ordered
3. Wear N95 masks or wet cloth to filter ash
4. Protect water sources from ash contamination
5. Do not return home until the all-clear is given
