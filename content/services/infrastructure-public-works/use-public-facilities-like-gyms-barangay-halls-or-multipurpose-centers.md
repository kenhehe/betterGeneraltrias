# Use Public Facilities like Gyms, Barangay Halls, or Multipurpose Centers

Tagaytay City maintains public facilities that residents can use for community activities, sports, events, and other purposes.

## Available Public Facilities

### Tolentino Sports and Activity Center (TSAC)

The main sports complex of Tagaytay City.

- Basketball court / covered courts
- Open grounds for events
- Also serves as an evacuation center during disasters

**Contact:** (046) 413-1220 (City Hall)

### Barangay Halls and Multipurpose Centers

Each of Tagaytay's 34 barangays has a hall that may be used for:

- Community meetings
- Skills training and seminars
- Livelihood activities
- Voting precincts

Contact your barangay captain to reserve.

### Public Parks and Open Spaces

- **People's Park in the Sky** — scenic viewpoint and recreation area
- **Picnic Grove** — for family outings (entrance fee applies)
- **Barangay sports courts** — for basketball and other sports

## How to Reserve a Facility

1. Visit the City Hall or the concerned barangay hall
2. Fill out a facility use request form
3. Get approval from the City Mayor's Office or barangay captain
4. Pay any applicable facility fee

## Fees

Some facilities are free for government and community activities. Fees may apply for private events. Check with the **City Mayor's Office** or **General Services Office** for current rates.
