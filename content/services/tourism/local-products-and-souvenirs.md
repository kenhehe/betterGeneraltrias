# Local Products & Souvenirs — General Trias City

Support local businesses and bring home authentic General Trias products — from fresh dairy treats to handcrafted goods made by local artisans and livelihood programs.

> Source: [generaltrias.gov.ph/tourism/local-products-and-souvenirs](https://generaltrias.gov.ph/tourism/local-products-and-souvenirs)

---

## Food & Dairy Products

### GenTri's Best Dairy Products
Fresh, locally produced dairy items made from carabao milk — one of the signature products of General Trias City.

- **Products:** Carabao milk yogurt, carabao milk ice cream, fresh carabao milk
- **Category:** Dairy / Food Products

---

### Noraben Food Manufacturing
A local food manufacturer producing specialty food products in General Trias City.

- **Category:** Food Manufacturing

---

### Eden's Pastillas
Handcrafted pastillas — a classic Filipino sweet milk candy — made locally in General Trias City. A popular pasalubong item for visitors.

- **Category:** Confectionery / Pasalubong

---

## Handicrafts & Handmade Goods

### General Trias City Jail Female Dormitory — Livelihood Crafts
Products made by inmates through the jail's livelihood program, supporting rehabilitation and skills development.

- **Products:** Handmade bags, handicrafts
- **Category:** Handicrafts / Livelihood Products

---

### General Trias City Jail Male Dormitory — Livelihood Crafts
Artworks and functional crafts produced by male inmates as part of the facility's livelihood and rehabilitation program.

- **Products:** Paintings, metal crafts, wood works
- **Category:** Arts & Crafts / Livelihood Products

---

## Where to Buy

Local products are available at:
- City-sponsored trade fairs and business expos
- Local markets within General Trias City
- Direct from producers — contact the City Tourism Office for referrals
