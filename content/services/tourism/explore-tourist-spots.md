# Must-See Places in General Trias City

Discover the official tourist destinations of General Trias City, Cavite — from centuries-old churches to championship golf and lush public parks.

> Source: [generaltrias.gov.ph/tourism/must-see-places](https://generaltrias.gov.ph/tourism/must-see-places)

---

## 1. Our Lady of Guadalupe Parish Church

A beloved Catholic parish and the spiritual heart of the community. Hosts major religious celebrations, processions, and community events throughout the year.

- **Location:** Crisanto M. De los Reyes Ave., General Trias, Cavite
- **Contact:** (046) 433-0544
- **Category:** Religious & Historical
- **Admission:** Free

---

## 2. San Francisco de Malabon Parish Church

Built by Franciscan missionaries in **1611**, this is one of the oldest churches in Cavite. A national historical landmark — the Philippine national anthem was first performed here during the June 12, 1898 independence proclamation.

- **Location:** Governor Ferrer Dr., Sampalucan, General Trias, Cavite
- **Contact:** 0976 036 5475
- **Category:** Religious & Historical
- **Admission:** Free

---

## 3. GBR Museum

Located inside the Gateway Business Park, the GBR Museum showcases exhibits on local history, culture, and heritage — offering a deeper look into the rich story of General Trias City.

- **Location:** Gateway Business Park, Barangay Javalera, General Trias City, Cavite
- **Contact:** (046) 885-7027
- **Category:** Museum & Culture

---

## 4. General Trias City Park

A central public park in the heart of the city — ideal for morning jogs, family picnics, and community gatherings.

- **Location:** General Trias City Park, General Trias, Cavite
- **Category:** Parks & Recreation
- **Admission:** Free

---

## 5. Eagle Ridge Golf & Country Club

A premier **36-hole championship golf course** in Barangay Javalera, regarded as one of the finest golf clubs in the Philippines.

- **Location:** Brgy. Javalera, City of General Trias, Cavite
- **Contact:** 09171220723
- **Website:** https://eagle-ridge.com.ph/
- **Category:** Leisure & Sports

---

## 6. City of General Trias People's Park

A well-maintained public park in Barangay Biclatan offering open spaces, walking paths, and recreational amenities for all ages.

- **Location:** Brgy. Biclatan, General Trias, Cavite
- **Category:** Parks & Recreation
- **Admission:** Free
