# Apply for Tourism-Related Business Permits — Tagaytay City

Tagaytay City is a premier tourist destination. If you are setting up a tourism-related business (hotel, resort, restaurant, tour operator, souvenir shop, etc.), you need to secure the proper permits and accreditations from the city government.

---

## Step 1: Secure a Barangay Business Clearance

Before going to the City Hall, obtain a **Barangay Business Clearance** from the barangay where your business will operate.

**Requirements:**

- Duly accomplished application form
- Proof of business address (lease contract or land title)
- Valid government ID of the owner
- Barangay clearance fee (varies per barangay)

---

## Step 2: Apply for a Mayor's Business Permit (BPLO)

Go to the **Business Permit and Licensing Office (BPLO)** at Tagaytay City Hall.

**Location:** Akle St., Kaybagal South, Tagaytay City, Cavite
**Contact:** (046) 888-9500
**Hours:** Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM–5:00 PM

**Requirements for New Applications:**

- Accomplished Business Permit Application Form
- DTI/SEC/CDA registration (depending on business type)
- Barangay Business Clearance
- Lease contract or proof of land ownership
- Community Tax Certificate (CTC)
- Valid government ID of owner/authorized representative
- Fire Safety Inspection Certificate (FSIC) from Bureau of Fire Protection
- Sanitary Permit from the City Health Office
- Zoning Clearance from the City Planning and Development Office

**Processing time:** 3–5 working days (new applications)

---

## Step 3: Secure a Fire Safety Inspection Certificate (FSIC)

Before your Mayor's Permit can be released, the **Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP)** must inspect your premises.

**Contact (BFP Tagaytay):** (046) 483-1193 / (046) 471-3747

---

## Step 4: Department of Tourism (DOT) Accreditation (Optional but Recommended)

For tourist accommodation establishments, tour operators, and restaurants, securing **DOT accreditation** is recommended and may be required for certain transactions.

Visit the **DOT Region IV-A (CALABARZON) office** or apply online at: https://dot.gov.ph

---

## Renewal of Business Permits

Annual renewal is required before **January 20** of each year to avoid penalties.

**Renewal requirements:**

- Previous year's business permit
- BIR Annual Registration (Form 0605)
- Updated barangay clearance
- Updated FSIC and sanitary permit
- Accomplished renewal form
