# Get Veterinary Services for Livestock

The General Trias City Agriculture Office provides free and subsidized veterinary services for livestock and poultry to support the city's farmers and backyard raisers.

## Services Available

- **Vaccination** — hog cholera, Newcastle disease, rabies (for dogs and cats), and other animal diseases
- **Deworming** — for cattle, goats, pigs, and poultry
- **Castration** — for male livestock
- **Treatment** — basic diagnosis and treatment for sick animals
- **Animal health consultation** — advice on proper livestock management

## Who Can Avail

- General Trias City residents with livestock or poultry
- Registered farmers and backyard animal raisers
- Pet owners (for rabies vaccination campaigns)

## Requirements

- Valid government-issued ID
- Proof of residence in General Trias City

## How to Avail

1. Visit the City Agriculture Office or call to schedule a visit
2. For large animals (cattle, carabao, horses), request a farm visit
3. Bring small animals directly to the office on scheduled veterinary days

## Where to Go

**City Agriculture Office**
General Trias City Hall, Akle St., Kaybagal South, General Trias City
Telephone: (046) 413-1220

## Schedule

Veterinary services are available on scheduled days. Vaccination campaigns are announced through barangay halls and city social media pages.
