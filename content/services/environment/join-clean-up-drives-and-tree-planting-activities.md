# Join Clean-Up Drives and Tree Planting Activities

Tagaytay City regularly organizes environmental programs including community clean-up drives and tree planting activities to preserve the city's natural beauty and protect Taal Lake's ecosystem.

## How to Participate

1. Watch for announcements from the City Environment and Natural Resources Office (CENRO) or the City Public Information Office (PIO)
2. Register your name and contact number at the barangay hall or CENRO office
3. Arrive at the designated area on the scheduled date
4. Bring your own gloves, face mask, and water if possible

## Scheduled Activities

- **Monthly Barangay Clean-Up** — Last Saturday of every month, coordinated by each barangay
- **City-Wide Clean-Up** — Every third Friday of the month (Brigada Kalikasan)
- **Tree Planting** — June (Environment Month) and other designated dates

## Where to Register

**City Environment and Natural Resources Office (CENRO)**
Tagaytay City Hall, Akle St., Kaybagal South
Telephone: (046) 413-1220

## What to Bring

- Gloves (rubber or gardening gloves)
- Face mask
- Sturdy footwear
- Drinking water
- Garbage bags (optional, provided by the LGU)

## Benefits

- Certificate of participation for students needing community service hours
- Tagaytay City eco-volunteer recognition
- Free seedlings for participants in tree planting events

## Contact

For scheduling and coordination, contact the **City Public Information Office (PIO)** at (046) 413-1220 or visit the Tagaytay City Hall.
