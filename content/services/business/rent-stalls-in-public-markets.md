# Rent Stalls in Public Markets — General Trias City

General Trias City operates public markets where residents and traders can rent stalls to sell goods. The City Market Division under the City Treasurer's Office manages stall rental, assignments, and fees.

---

## Public Markets in General Trias City

### Kaybagal Public Market
The main public market serving the city center, offering a variety of fresh produce, meat, fish, dry goods, and cooked food sections.

- **Location:** Kaybagal South, General Trias City, Cavite
- **Operating Hours:** Daily, 5:00 AM – 7:00 PM
- **Section:** Wet market, dry goods, cooked food, RTW

---

## How to Rent a Stall

### Requirements

- Barangay Clearance (from your barangay of residence)
- Valid government-issued ID (with photo and signature)
- 2x2 ID photos (2 copies)
- Duly accomplished application form (available at the City Treasurer's Office)
- Business Permit application (filed simultaneously or after stall assignment)

---

## Application Process

| Step | What to Do | Where |
| ---- | ---------- | ----- |
| 1 | Get and fill out the stall application form | City Treasurer's Office |
| 2 | Submit requirements and await stall availability | City Market Division |
| 3 | Receive Notice of Stall Assignment | City Treasurer's Office |
| 4 | Pay stall rental fee and get Official Receipt | City Treasurer's Office |
| 5 | Apply for Business Permit at the Business Permits Office | City Hall |

---

## Stall Rental Fees

Stall rental fees are set by the Sangguniang Panlungsod (City Council) through an ordinance and are subject to change. Contact the City Treasurer's Office for current rates.

- **Daily rental fee:** Based on stall classification and location within the market
- **Monthly rental:** Available for permanent stall holders
- **Penalties:** Late payment of rental fees may result in surcharges per city ordinance

---

## Rules and Regulations

- Stall holders must maintain cleanliness and orderliness in and around their assigned stall
- Sub-leasing or transferring of stalls without approval is prohibited
- Business Permit must be displayed at all times
- Stall holders must comply with sanitary regulations of the City Health Office
