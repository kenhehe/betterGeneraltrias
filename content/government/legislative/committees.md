# Standing Committees — Sangguniang Panlungsod

The **Sangguniang Panlungsod ng Tagaytay City** operates through standing committees that review, deliberate, and report on proposed ordinances and resolutions before they are voted on in plenary session.

---

## Standing Committees

| Committee                                      | Jurisdiction                                                                 |
| ---------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Committee on Appropriations                    | City budget, supplemental appropriations, financial allocations              |
| Committee on Laws and Ordinances               | Review and drafting of city ordinances, legal matters                        |
| Committee on Health and Sanitation             | Public health programs, sanitation, RHU services                             |
| Committee on Tourism                           | Tourism promotion, accreditation of tourism establishments, visitor services |
| Committee on Environment and Natural Resources | Environmental protection, tree planting, quarrying, waste management         |
| Committee on Education                         | School programs, scholarships, day care, and LGU education support           |
| Committee on Public Works and Infrastructure   | Roads, bridges, drainage, public buildings                                   |
| Committee on Peace and Order                   | Public safety, BPSO, Barangay Peacekeeping, disaster response                |
| Committee on Ways and Means                    | Revenue-generating measures, local taxation                                  |
| Committee on Trade and Commerce                | Business permits, market operations, livelihood programs                     |
| Committee on Social Services                   | Social welfare, senior citizens, PWD, solo parents, relief assistance        |
| Committee on Agriculture and Fisheries         | Farming support, veterinary services, fisherfolk programs                    |
| Committee on Housing and Land Use              | Socialized housing, relocation, zoning, land use planning                    |

---

## How Committees Work

1. A proposed ordinance or resolution is referred by the **Vice Mayor** to the relevant committee
2. The committee holds **public hearings** if the measure affects the general public
3. The committee submits a **committee report** recommending approval, amendment, or disapproval
4. The full City Council votes in **plenary session**
