# City Council Resolutions — General Trias City

Resolutions are official declarations or expressions of intent by the **Sangguniang Panlungsod ng General Trias City**. Unlike ordinances, resolutions are typically one-time acts that do not carry the force of permanent law.

---

## What is a Resolution?

A resolution is a formal decision adopted by the City Council on a specific matter — such as authorizing a contract, expressing gratitude or condolences, endorsing a national program, or requesting action from another government body. Resolutions do not require the Mayor's signature to take effect unless otherwise required by law.

---

## Common Types of Resolutions

| Type                     | Purpose                                                             |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Authorization Resolution | Authorizes the Mayor to enter into contracts or agreements          |
| Appropriation Resolution | Approves emergency or supplemental appropriations                   |
| Recognition Resolution   | Commends or recognizes individuals, groups, or organizations        |
| Endorsement Resolution   | Supports or endorses programs, national bills, or external requests |
| Request Resolution       | Requests action from national agencies or other LGUs                |

---

## Access Resolution Records

Resolutions are recorded in the official **legislative records** of the Sangguniang Panlungsod. Certified copies are available at the Secretariat.
