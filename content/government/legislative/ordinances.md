# City Ordinances — General Trias City

City ordinances are local laws enacted by the **Sangguniang Panlungsod ng General Trias City**. They govern matters of public policy, public order, and the general welfare of the city's residents and visitors.

---

## What is a City Ordinance?

An ordinance is a formal piece of legislation passed by the City Council (Sangguniang Panlungsod) and signed into law by the **City Mayor**. Ordinances are binding on all residents, businesses, and visitors within General Trias City's jurisdiction.

---

## How Ordinances are Passed

1. A councilor or the Mayor files a proposed ordinance
2. The presiding officer refers it to the relevant **standing committee**
3. The committee holds deliberations and, if needed, a **public hearing**
4. The committee submits its report to the full council
5. The ordinance is voted on in **plenary session** (two readings required)
6. If approved, it is transmitted to the **Mayor** for signature or veto
7. Signed ordinances take effect after publication or posting

---

## Access Ordinance Records

The full text of city ordinances is on file at the **Sangguniang Panlungsod Secretariat**, City Hall. Certified copies may be requested for legal, business, or academic purposes.
