# City Ordinances — Tagaytay City

**Ordinances** are local laws enacted by the **Sangguniang Panlungsod** (City Council) of Tagaytay City. They have the force of law within the city and are signed by the **Mayor** before taking effect.

---

## How Ordinances Are Made

1. **Filing** — A councilor, the Mayor, or a citizens' group files a proposed ordinance
2. **Committee referral** — Referred to the appropriate committee of the Sangguniang Panlungsod
3. **Public hearing** — Mandatory for ordinances affecting the general public
4. **Committee report** — Submitted to the full council
5. **First and second readings** — Full council deliberates and votes
6. **Approval** — Requires majority vote; signed by the Vice Mayor (presiding officer)
7. **Mayor's action** — Mayor approves (signs) or vetoes within 15 days
8. **Effectivity** — Takes effect 10 days after posting in public places, unless otherwise stated

---

## Key Ordinance Categories

### Revenue and Taxation

- Local Revenue Code — sets taxes, fees, and charges within the city
- Real Property Tax ordinances

### Business Regulation

- Business permit requirements and procedures
- Regulation of tourism establishments (hotels, restaurants, resorts)
- Hawkers and market vendor regulations

### Land Use and Zoning

- Comprehensive Land Use Plan (CLUP) implementing ordinances
- Zoning and subdivision regulations
- Building code amendments

### Environment

- Solid waste management and anti-littering
- Tree-cutting and tree-planting regulations
- Air and noise pollution control

### Public Order and Safety

- Anti-smoking ordinances
- Curfew ordinances
- Liquor regulation

### Social and Welfare

- Senior citizen benefits and privileges
- PWD (Persons with Disability) assistance
- Solo parent support

### Tourism

- Tourism zone regulations
- Tourist-related business standards
