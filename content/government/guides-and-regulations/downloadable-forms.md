## Where to Submit

Bring completed forms to the relevant office at **Tagaytay City Hall**, Akle St., Kaybagal South, Tagaytay City.

For inquiries: **(046) 888-9500** (connecting all offices) or **(046) 413-1220**

> All downloadable forms are sourced from the [Official Tagaytay City Government Website](https://www.tagaytay.gov.ph/downloads.html). Check there for the most up-to-date versions.
