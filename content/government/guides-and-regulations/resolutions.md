# Sangguniang Panlungsod Resolutions — Tagaytay City

**Resolutions** are formal expressions of the sense or opinion of the **Sangguniang Panlungsod** (City Council). Unlike ordinances, resolutions do not have the force of law — they express the council's position, authorize specific actions, or convey requests to other government bodies.

---

## Types of Resolutions

| Type                          | Purpose                                                                   |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Authorization Resolutions** | Authorize the Mayor to enter contracts, apply for loans, accept donations |
| **Appropriation Resolutions** | Authorize specific expenditures from the city's funds                     |
| **Commendation Resolutions**  | Recognize individuals, organizations, or achievements                     |
| **Request Resolutions**       | Formally request action from national agencies (DPWH, DepEd, DOH, etc.)   |
| **Position Resolutions**      | Express the city council's stance on legislative or policy matters        |
| **Emergency Resolutions**     | Authorize emergency measures during calamities or disasters               |

---

## How Resolutions Are Passed

1. **Filing** — Councilor files the proposed resolution
2. **Committee referral** (if needed) — For complex matters
3. **Full council deliberation** — Discussion and amendments
4. **Vote** — Majority vote required for approval
5. **Signing** — Signed by the presiding officer (Vice Mayor)
6. **Transmittal** — Sent to the Mayor for appropriate action (if authorization is required)

## Recent Notable Resolution Areas

The Sangguniang Panlungsod regularly passes resolutions covering:

- Endorsement of infrastructure projects for national funding (DPWH, DILG)
- Authorization for the Mayor to sign contracts for city projects
- Recognition of outstanding Tagaytay City residents, athletes, and organizations
- Disaster declarations and emergency authorizations (e.g., Taal Volcano eruption responses)
- Endorsement of scholarship recipients
- Acceptance of donations and grants for city programs

---

## Legislative Calendar

The Sangguniang Panlungsod holds **regular sessions** and may hold **special sessions** when urgent matters arise:

- Regular sessions: At least once a week (schedule posted at City Hall)
- Sessions are open to the public unless declared executive session
- Minutes of sessions are public records
