# Annual Budget — Tagaytay City

The **Annual Budget** (also called the **Annual Appropriations Ordinance**) is the city's spending plan for the fiscal year, enacted by the **Sangguniang Panlungsod** and signed by the **Mayor**.

Under the **Full Disclosure Policy**, the city is required to post the approved annual budget within **30 days of enactment**.

---

## Budget Sources

Tagaytay City's budget is funded from:

| Source                               | Description                                                        |
| ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Internal Revenue Allotment (IRA)** | Share of national taxes — the primary funding source for most LGUs |
| **Local Taxes**                      | Business taxes, real property tax, fees and charges                |
| **Non-Tax Revenue**                  | Permits, licenses, market fees, utility income                     |
| **Grants and Subsidies**             | National government grants, foreign-assisted projects              |

---

## Budget Allocation (General Structure)

The city budget is allocated across the following major areas:

| Expenditure Type            | Description                                                |
| --------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| **General Public Services** | City administration, legislative, finance, legal           |
| **Health Services**         | City Health Office, Ospital ng Tagaytay operations         |
| **Education**               | Special Education Fund (SEF), scholarship programs         |
| **Social Services**         | CSWDO programs for seniors, PWDs, indigent families        |
| **Economic Services**       | Agriculture, business development, tourism                 |
| **Infrastructure**          | Roads, drainage, public facilities, DPWH-assisted projects |
| **Debt Service**            | Loan repayments (if any)                                   |
| **20% Development Fund**    | Mandatory development projects from IRA                    |
| **5% DRRMF**                | Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Fund                |
| **1% GAD Fund**             | Gender and Development programs                            |

## Budget Cycle

| Step                                      | Timeline                        |
| ----------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Executive prepares budget proposal        | July–August                     |
| Mayor submits to Sangguniang Panlungsod   | On or before October 16         |
| Sangguniang Panlungsod deliberates        | October–November                |
| Budget enacted (Appropriations Ordinance) | On or before December 31        |
| Budget takes effect                       | January 1 of the following year |

---

## Quarterly Financial Reports

In addition to the annual budget, the city publishes **Quarterly Financial Reports** covering income and expenditures. These are also available at the FDP Portal.
