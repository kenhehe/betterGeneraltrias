# Statement of Assets, Liabilities and Net Worth (SALN) — Tagaytay City

The **SALN** is a sworn declaration that all government officials and employees are required to file annually under **Republic Act 6713** (Code of Conduct and Ethical Standards for Public Officials and Employees) and **Republic Act 3019** (Anti-Graft and Corrupt Practices Act).

SALNs promote transparency and accountability by disclosing the financial interests of public servants.

---

## Who Must File

All **elected officials**, **department heads**, and **government employees** of Tagaytay City are required to file a SALN, including:

- Mayor
- Vice Mayor
- City Councilors (Sangguniang Panlungsod members)
- Department heads and division chiefs
- All plantilla employees

---

## Filing Schedule

| Filing                       | Deadline                                                        |
| ---------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Annual SALN                  | On or before **April 30** of each year (for the preceding year) |
| Upon assumption of office    | Within **30 days** of taking office                             |
| Upon separation from service | Within **30 days** of separation                                |

---

## What is Disclosed

Each SALN must include:

- **Assets** — real property (land, buildings), personal property (vehicles, bank accounts, investments, business interests), and other assets
- **Liabilities** — loans, mortgages, and other financial obligations
- **Net Worth** — total assets minus total liabilities
- **Business interests and financial connections**
- **Relatives in government** within the fourth degree of consanguinity or affinity

---

## How to Access SALN Records

Under **Executive Order 2 (2016)** (Freedom of Information), SALNs of government officials are public records.

**Request a copy:**

1. File a written request at the **Office of the Human Resource Management Officer (HRMO)**, Tagaytay City Hall
2. Specify the name of the official and the year(s) requested
3. Or file via the national FOI portal: https://www.foi.gov.ph

**Ombudsman SALN Portal (for national officials):** https://www.ombudsman.gov.ph

**Contact — City HRMO:**
Tagaytay City Hall, Akle St., Kaybagal South, Tagaytay City, Cavite
Tel: (046) 888-9500
