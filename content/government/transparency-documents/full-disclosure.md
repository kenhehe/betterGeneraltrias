# Full Disclosure Policy Documents — Tagaytay City

Under **DILG Memorandum Circular 2013-140** and subsequent issuances, all local government units are required to post key fiscal and financial documents in publicly accessible locations and on their official website.

Tagaytay City complies with the **Full Disclosure Policy (FDP)** by publishing these documents at:

- City Hall bulletin boards (Akle St., Kaybagal South, Tagaytay City)
- The official website: https://www.tagaytay.gov.ph
- The DILG FDP Portal: https://fdpp.blgs.gov.ph

---

## Required FDP Documents

### Budget Documents

| Document                         | Description                                                                     | Period              |
| -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------- |
| Annual Budget (AIP)              | Annual Investment Program — all programs, projects, and activities with funding | Current fiscal year |
| Supplemental Budget              | Supplemental appropriations, if any                                             | As enacted          |
| 20% Development Fund Utilization | How the 20% of IRA allotted for development is spent                            | Quarterly           |
| Trust Fund Utilization           | Receipts and disbursements of trust funds                                       | Quarterly           |

### Financial Statements

| Document                               | Description                                            | Period    |
| -------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | --------- |
| Statement of Receipts and Expenditures | Summary of all income and spending                     | Quarterly |
| Statement of Cash Flow                 | Cash inflows and outflows                              | Quarterly |
| Balance Sheet                          | Assets, liabilities, and equity of the city government | Annual    |
| Statement of Changes in Equity         | Changes in government equity                           | Annual    |

### Procurement Documents

| Document                       | Description                                                 |
| ------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| Bid Results (Goods & Services) | Results of public bidding for goods and consulting services |
| Bid Results (Infrastructure)   | Results of public bidding for infrastructure projects       |
| Annual Procurement Plan (APP)  | Planned procurements for the fiscal year                    |
| Contracts Awarded              | Summary of contracts awarded above the threshold            |

### Other Required Disclosures

| Document                            | Description                                            |
| ----------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Manpower Complement                 | List of all city government employees and positions    |
| SEF Utilization                     | How the Special Education Fund is used                 |
| DRRMF Utilization                   | Use of the Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Fund |
| Gender and Development (GAD) Budget | GAD fund utilization                                   |

## How to Request Documents

If documents are not yet posted online, residents may file a **Freedom of Information (FOI) request**:

1. Visit the **City Hall** (Akle St., Kaybagal South, Tagaytay City)
2. Submit a written request at the **Office of the City Administrator** or the relevant department
3. Or file online at: https://www.foi.gov.ph

**Contact:** (046) 888-9500

## Transparency Seal

Tagaytay City holds the **Transparency Seal**, awarded to LGUs that comply with public disclosure requirements. The seal is displayed on the official website.
