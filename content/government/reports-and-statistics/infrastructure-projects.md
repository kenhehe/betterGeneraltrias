# Infrastructure Projects — Tagaytay City

This page tracks **ongoing and recently completed infrastructure projects** funded by the City Government of Tagaytay, the national government, and other sources.

Under the **Full Disclosure Policy**, bid results for infrastructure projects and contract awards must be publicly posted.

---

## How to Check Project Status

Procurement documents, bid results, and contract awards are published at:

- **DILG FDP Portal:** https://fdpp.blgs.gov.ph (search "Tagaytay City")
- **PhilGEPS (Government Electronic Procurement System):** https://www.philgeps.gov.ph
- **Official Website:** https://www.tagaytay.gov.ph
- **City Engineering Office:** Tagaytay City Hall, Tel: (046) 888-9500

## Project Categories

The City Government funds infrastructure through:

| Funding Source                   | Project Types                                            |
| -------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| **20% Development Fund (IRA)**   | Barangay roads, drainage, multi-purpose halls            |
| **DRRMF (5% of IRA)**            | Flood control, evacuation centers, early warning systems |
| **General Fund**                 | City hall facilities, public markets, parks              |
| **DPWH (National)**              | National and provincial roads passing through the city   |
| **SEF (Special Education Fund)** | School buildings, classrooms, school facilities          |
| **LGU Loans / Bonds**            | Major capital projects (subject to Sangguniang approval) |

## Procurement Process

All infrastructure projects above the **Small Value Procurement** threshold undergo public bidding through the **Bids and Awards Committee (BAC)**:

1. **Project identification** — BAC prepares Annual Procurement Plan (APP)
2. **Advertisement** — Posted on PhilGEPS, city bulletin boards, and official website
3. **Pre-bid conference** — Open to all prospective bidders
4. **Bid submission and opening** — Public opening; results posted on FDP Portal
5. **Contract award** — Winning bidder notified; contract posted publicly
6. **Implementation** — With regular inspection by City Engineering Office
7. **Completion and turnover** — Documented and reported in Annual Accomplishment Report
